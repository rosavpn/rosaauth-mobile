Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 28, 2025

1. Data Collection

OwnAuth is designed with privacy as its core principle. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, usage analytics, or tracking data.

2. Data Storage

All authentication tokens and secrets are stored locally on your device. If you enable Cloud Sync, your data is encrypted on your device before being sent to your self-hosted server.

3. Third-Party Services

The application engages with no third-party services for analytics, advertising, or tracking. The only network connections made are to the sync server URL you explicitly provide.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
